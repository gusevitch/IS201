Profits Across the United States

As detailed above, we can see that we have earned the highest profit in the states of New York and California. This could be expected due to high population density, however, it does not attribute to the loss of profit in the peculiar state of Texas. We also see high potentional and success in Washington.


California's Sales in the Past 2 Years

California's sales have flucuated across the months, but we are able to see a gradual incline in profit. Interestingly, profit peaks in the later months of the year, even after the start of school (which usually occurs in July/August).


Success with Product Category

We have found the most success in the "office supplies" category in sales. Looking at this chart, we see our highest profiting state, Califonira, leading sales with Office Supplies. It can be assumed that general office supplies, although low in individual price, is sold the most in number, and has obviously led to the majority of our success.


Forecasting Sales of Office Supplies

Sales of office supplies are forecasted to stay a little over $5k for the state of California in the upcoming months. This causes us to believe similar patterns can be expected from other states. We can prevent such lack of profit if we begin now to implement a plan to boost office supply sales, such as through advertisment, promotions, and so on.
