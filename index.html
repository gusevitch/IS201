Welcome to My Life!

Gabby Usevitch 101

Welcome to this rundown of my life!
Let's start with a quick lil' life sketch:)

July 5, 2000


I was born in Gilbert, Arizona on July 5th, 2000.
Fun fact- my mom went into labor while she was watching the fireworks on the 4th;)

Family


As for me- I'm the youngest in a family of 8! I have my parents, 4 older brothers, and 1 older sister. All of my brother have gotten married, so I now also have 4 sister in-laws, along with 5 nephews, and 2 nieces.

April, 2018


I graduated high school and headed off to my freshman year at BYU

The other person is my brother Zach, who graduated with his masters at the same time I graduated high school! So of course we took graduation pictures together!

December, 2019


After my freshman year, I decided I wanted to serve a mission and got called to the Hungary, Romania mission - Hungarian speaking! I worked full time for a couple of months, and then reported to the Missionary Training Center in December of 2019.

June, 2021


I came home from Hungary in June of 2021! Luckily, I didn't have to go home in the middle of my mission, nor get reassigned due to covid. I was definitley one of the lucky ones, but oh was it fantastic to see my family again;)

Present!


I'm now back at BYU, and just finished my sophomore year! I'm studying EXDM, and want to work in Parks & Recreation! Besides that though, I'm working and just try to go on as many trips as I can. Holler:D
